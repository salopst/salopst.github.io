# Τέρτυλλος, ου, ὁ

Tertullos  
Noun, Masculine  
*Tertullus, a barrister acting as professional prosecutor of Paul at Caesarea.*  
Strong's: **5061**  

2 occurrences in GNT

```text
Τέρτυλλος (N-NMS), Acts 24:2  
Τερτύλλου (N-GMS), Acts 24:1  
```
